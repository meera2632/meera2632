# 👋 Hi, I'm Meera

**I turn messy, real-world data into systems that actually work in production**, whether that's a pipeline processing 50K+ records a day, a model that needs to survive contact with reality, or a dashboard someone actually opens twice.

I recently completed my MS in Applied Data Science at San Jose State University, and across 3 internships and a research role, I've bounced between data engineering, ML, and analytics, not because I couldn't decide, but because I kept noticing the same thing: **the best data people understand the whole pipeline, not just their slice of it.**

---

## 🧠 What I Build

I like problems that sit between disciplines:

- **ETL/ELT pipelines** that don't fall over under real-world messiness (missing labels, noisy signals, multi-source chaos)
- **ML systems** - from training to deployment to *actually monitoring what happens after*
- **LLM-powered agentic workflows** that automate the boring parts of a pipeline
- **Full-stack & backend systems** - REST/GraphQL APIs, microservices, and the plumbing that gets a model or pipeline in front of real users
- **Dashboards & reporting** that translate "here's 50K rows" into "here's what to do about it"

A few things I've shipped: a graph-intelligence platform over 500K+ enterprise emails with a multi-agent retrieval system, a computer vision pipeline that pushed shoplifting detection accuracy from 62%→92%, a distributed full-stack travel platform with event-driven microservices on AWS, and a reinforcement-learning-augmented acoustic detection system that cut false positives by 66%.
